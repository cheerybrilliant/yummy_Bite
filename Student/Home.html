ICTU Online Canteen

ICTU Online Canteen is a food order management system for campus meals. It helps students view available dishes, place orders, upload Mobile Money payment proof, track preparation, and collect meals when the kitchen marks them ready.

View today's menu Create student account

Meals managed in the system

The canteen menu is organized by meal type so students can quickly find common dishes, snacks, soups, and drinks. Admins can add new meals, update prices, upload food images, and publish what is available each day.

Main meals

Ndole and Plantain, Jollof Rice with Chicken, Poulet DG, Eru and Water Fufu, and other full lunch meals.

Soups

Achu and Yellow Soup, Fish Pepper Soup, and other soup dishes the kitchen can prepare for students.

Snacks

Puff-Puff, Beignet-Haricot, Fried Plantain, and quick meals students can order between lectures.

Drinks

Folere, Bissap, bottled water, and other refreshments managed with clear prices and availability.

What the service does

Browse meals

Students view the daily menu, prices, stock, and meal details from the database.

Order online

Students add food to the cart and generate an Order ID before payment.

Upload proof

Students pay the canteen MoMo number on their phone, then upload screenshot proof and transaction ID.

Track pickup

The kitchen verifies payment, prepares food, marks it ready, and students confirm collection.

Who uses it

Students

Create an account, order meals, upload payment proof, track status, vote weekly, and review food.

Cooks

Verify payments, move orders through preparation, mark meals ready, and approve meal suggestions.

Admins

Manage dishes, upload meal images, publish the daily menu, create cooks, and check analytics.
