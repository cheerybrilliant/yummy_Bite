About ICTU Online Canteen

ICTU Online Canteen was built to manage student food orders in a simple and organized way. Instead of students waiting in long lines or guessing when food is ready, the app lets them see the daily menu, place an order, upload payment proof, and track the order until pickup.

The system also helps the canteen staff. Cooks can verify Mobile Money payment proof, see confirmed orders, prepare meals, mark food as ready, and keep a clear record of completed transactions.

Admins use the system to manage dishes, prices, meal images, daily stock, cooks, votes, suggestions, and order analytics.

Dishes on the platform

The menu is built around common campus meals and Cameroonian dishes. Admins can add more dishes, update prices, upload real meal images, and decide what is available each day.

Main meals

Examples include Ndole and Plantain, Jollof Rice with Chicken, Poulet DG, Eru and Water Fufu, and other filling lunch meals.

Soups

Meals such as Achu and Yellow Soup or Fish Pepper Soup can be added to the daily menu when available.

Snacks

Students can order quick options like Puff-Puff, Beignet-Haricot, Fried Plantain, and other canteen snacks.

Drinks

The canteen can list drinks like Folere, Bissap, bottled water, and other refreshments with their prices.

Purpose of the app

For students

Create an account, browse meals, order food, upload payment proof, track cooking status, confirm collection, vote, and review meals.

For cooks

Verify payments, manage confirmed tickets, prepare meals, mark orders ready, and approve meal suggestions they can cook.

For admins

Control dishes, daily menu, stock, meal photos, staff accounts, voting results, and canteen records.
