About us

The ICT University Online Canteen

This platform helps students order food before reaching the canteen. Students create accounts, choose meals, upload Mobile Money proof, track cooking status, and confirm collection.

Staff verify payment screenshots, prepare orders, mark meals ready, and review weekly meal suggestions.

Admins manage dishes, daily menu, cooks, votes, and analytics.
